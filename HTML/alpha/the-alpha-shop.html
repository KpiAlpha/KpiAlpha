Alpha Phi Alpha

A Landmark Development in African American College Student Life

Alpha men birthed the Civil Rights Movement. They founded and supported institutions such as the National Association for the Advancement of Colored People, The Crisis magazine, the National Urban League and the National Association of Black Journalists. An Alpha man successfully argued the landmark Brown v. Board of Education case before the Supreme Court that eradicated school segregation.

Ours is the only fraternity whose members dreamed to erect the only memorial to an American born of African decent in the nation’s capital. Today, Alpha Men build stronger communities through their activism and mentoring initiatives.
